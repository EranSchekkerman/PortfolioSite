I do art as well!

Hi there!

My name is Eran and I am a narrative & 3D designer from the Netherlands. I recently graduated from the Hanze UAS with a bachelor in Game Design.

Art and storytelling have always been incredibly important to me. I try to thread the needle in my work by utilising more tools than text to convey a story. After all, text is only one tool in the infinite toolbox of storytelling. My ultimate goal is to make immersive products that people can get lost in, grow from and think about long after they stopped playing.

I often spend my free time in the boulderhall, in a dusty room playing Dungeons & Dragons, or working on my projects whilst keeping my cat from desperately trying to sample the newest flavour of houseplant I just bought.
