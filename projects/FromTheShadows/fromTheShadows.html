From The Shadows

About Game

From The Shadows is a horror top-down game in which you are tasked with trying to rescue an old friend from a deserved island. Whilst rescuing this friend you notice this island isn't as deserted as you thought.

This project was created in a school project of 8

Made in Unity

Narrative Writing

After a lot of revisions and scale-downs I managed to make a concise narrative that suited our small unpublished demo. The story centers around the theme of the past repeating itself and includes 6 characters, 4 of which being characters from previous expeditions that leave audio logs the player can find.

Cinematic Trailer Creation

This trailer was created within 24 hours. I roughly rendered everything in Blender and voice acted a snippet of dialogue I wrote. The overal purpose of the trailer was to give the player a sense of the mystery that was waiting for them in the collapsed mines of the island.

I mostly used composition of thirds when creating the renders. Some of the angles are meant to convey a sense of hopelessness in front of problems bigger than one's self. Others like the door render I created, were created with the intention of getting the player curious, and giving them a promise of the narrative importance of the door, and what secrets lie behind it. It is essentially portrayed the tsjechovs gun of the story.

Environmental Design

I did a chunk of the level design and environmental dressing, mostly situated around the ruined village. When creating the terrain and placing the props, I made sure the seperate sections of the village in a way that suited the narrative build up and storyline.
