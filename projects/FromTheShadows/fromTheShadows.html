From The Shadows

About Game

From The Shadows is a horror top-down game in which you are tasked with trying to rescue an old friend from a deserved island. Whilst rescuing this friend you notice this island isn't as deserted as you thought.

Made in Unity

Narrative Writing

The main project was made and edited in Arcweave. I drew images and added sound effects, including voiced lines, to further prove the concept and add to the tone.

Click on the image to look at the demo.

Trailer Creation

To write a scene I always start with a solid goal in mind. This scene was meant to be the transition between acts, before the main character goes on a journey of death to reclaim the role of God of Death that was stolen from his master. Whilst writing I kept in mind the individual characters and their motivations to betray the main character and assasinate their mentor, adding more depth to their reaction than a simple 'Rhaaaa, they are evil gods!'.
